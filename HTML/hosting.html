Alice is "hosting" her verification

Alice (as the Host) is presented with a communication page to set up her meeting with Bob


All communication is encrypted between Alice and Bob but the "Watchman" can review their interactions like a "man in the middle"


This is to ensure that they do not conspire to exchange their cryptographic "secrets" out of band. It also means that there will be an auditable log of the interaction should there ever be any problem with a meeting.


Though in this demo we show Alice and Bob being able to see one another's profile images and names these may in fact be anonymised until moments before they are actually due to meet.


If this were the case then Bob would be given direction to within 5 minutes travel of Alice and will not have the final destination revealed until there was too little time for him to try and work out if it would be safe for him to “bribe” her out of band. I personally feel that this is unnecessary as the danger of offering to bribe an honest party would be to risk considerable damage to ones own IÐ should the other party prove to be honest.
