DEMO

Alice arranges to “host” a “witness”

Here Alice chooses a date and time range or a day of the week and submits it as a limiting factor for the etherReal matching engine


EtherReal-IÐ then uses that information to create a pool of users large enough to allow for true statistical randomness. Alice is then allocated a witness, in this case Bob, who has chosen compatible time slots and is prepared to travel far enough to be considered as being in the same area.


This is a set and forget process. The matching engine will not call the user until it is the turn and the have a match created.
